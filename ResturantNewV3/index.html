Few words about us

ESCĀ hospitality group was established in 2017, launching its first ‘World Luxury Restaurant Award Winning restaurant ‘ESCĀ’ in the heart of Heliopolis. After 4 years of excellence dining with highly presented food quality, the group decided to extend in what it knows best and open 2 new restaurants in New Cairo, Egypt.
