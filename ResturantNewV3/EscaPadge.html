About ESCĀ

ESCĀ is a glamorous contemporary dining restaurant, located in the heart of Heliopolis, launched in 2017 making its first ESCĀ Hospitality’s restaurant chain. ESCĀ is inspired from the Latin culture specifying food or delicious dish prepared for the table, renovated with an Egyptian touch.

ESCĀ was an idea turned into a real life concept that was opened and driven by passion for food. Entering the market and making our way up, Hospitality has become an essential part of our group’s strategy.

We offers premium food quality with highest ingrediants & aesthetic precisions that result in an abundant dining experience. ESCĀ is well known for its ‘Steak’ dishes like, ‘The Classic Chateaubriand’ which is specially made for sharing because we have not forgotten about the romanticistics.
