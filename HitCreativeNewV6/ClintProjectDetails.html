Magdy Yacoub Heart Foundation

Brief:

Founded in 2008 by Sir Magdi Yacoub, the late Dr. Ahmed Zewail and Ambassador Mohamed Shaker, the Magdi Yacoub Heart Foundation (MYF) is an Egyptian NGO that is responsible for the strategic management and administration of the Aswan Heart Centre (AHC) and Aswan Heart Research Centre, offering state-of-the-art medical services to the Egyptian people, especially the underprivileged and vulnerable age-groups, completely free of charge for a better, healthier future.
