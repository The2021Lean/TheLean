Application Process

We are looking for non-traditional scalable businesses that have high growth potential. You should have at least: a minimal viable product (MVP), some sort of traction, and a core team of two founders with know-how in their prospective roles.

  • Apply online

  • Interview

  • Due Diligence

  • Investment Committee

  • Growth Program

Apply Online

Our rolling application is open to all startups. Apply here

Interview

You will receive an email from xyzcircle to notify you if your company has been selected for an initial interview. Once we get in touch, we’ll schedule an interview, where you’ll pitch to the xyzcircle internal team and we will discuss your business, market, and growth strategy..

Due Diligence

After the interview, accepted startups will go through our due diligence process, where they will meet with our team to have the chance to work on their business model, growth strategy, and financials. Due Diligence is the final stage of our selection process, and accepted startups will move directly to our Investment Committee..

Investment Committee

Our investment committee (IC) is composed of internal stakeholders and external industry and investment experts. You’ll get the chance to pitch your startup in front of this distinguished panel and qualify for investment.

Growth Program

Congratulations, you made it! Now the real work begins as your company joins xyzcircle’ growth program. You will be based out of our co-working space and have access to the hands-on mentorship and growth hacking you need to build thriving businesses before showcasing your progress to investors.
