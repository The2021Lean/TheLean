Anuta Provid Retail Store Owner
(Kiosk, Supermarket or Grocery)

Anuta application depends on an easy-to-use system, which provides a direct communication channel with the largest number of suppliers in one place, in addition to providing a quick solution to ordering products and comparing their prices in one place, then paying them in cash or installments and receiving them on time.

  • Account statement and invoices
  • All orders are in one place, which facilitates the weekly or monthly inventory process.
  • Easy reference to previous orders and invoices by day, month or year.
  • Pay bills online.
  • Obtaining financing / increasing purchasing power.
  • Appropriate pricing and forecasting of demand by product.
  • Notices from suppliers, loyalty schemes and direct offers.

Anouta ensures leadership for suppliers

Anuta application is a permanent partner for suppliers in their journey towards success, as it aims to increase sales and reduce resources and distribution costs, while constantly informing them of detailed reports on consumption and demand indicators in the market, which enhances their marketing plans to ensure leadership over competitors.

  • Digitizing the purchasing process: ordering system + digital payment.
  • Instant billing + tracking of orders by customer.
  • Eliminate cash handling and collection risks through scheduled orders and a digital payment solution.
  • Manage cash flow and collection efficiently.
  • Creation of new unbroken sales channels (funded orders).
  • Demand planning and analytics (pre-scheduled orders).
  • Data and detailed reports to study the market.
  • Targeting customers based on geographic location.
  • Take advantage of direct promotions.
  • Advertisement of discounts for a specified period of time to the full list of retailers or targets.
  • A tailored loyalty program.
  • Managing your sales team, tracking new acquisitions and logging in to geolocation.
