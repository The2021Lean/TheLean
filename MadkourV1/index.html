About Us Madkour

Madkour Group was established in 2015, embarking on 30 years of expertise in the energy sector throughout the MENA region, Madkour evolved prospered with a proven track record of delivering mega energy; infrastructure projects with high levels of excellence, reliability & forward-thinking.Our success has inspired us at Madkour to embrace a vision that is geared to providing innovative & unique solutions in establishing energy, infrastructure and industrial projects, in ways that would contribute to enhancing Egypt’s energy & infrastructure sectors to ultimately fuel economic growth.We have witnessed that technology, businesses,societies are tightly interrelated spheres that undergo rapid transformation.
