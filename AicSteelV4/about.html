AIC Organization About

at a glance

Being part of the Attieh Group of companies that was established in 1952, we have over 60 years of steel heritage and over 20 years of experience in the steel business. Since our production started in 1994, AIC has successfully fabricated over one million tons of steel with exports to GCC, America, Europe, Africa, and Asia. In serving the global market, we have established facilities in key regions of the KSA, UAE, and in-house specialized engineering center in Egypt with more than 250 Engineers.

Currently, AIC’s annual production capacity exceeds 430,000 MT, distributed as follows:

  • Structural Steel
  • Steel Towers
  • Platework
  • Platework

In addition to Galvanizing annual capacity of over 200,000 MT of steel products.

Message from
the founder founder

Welcome to the Arabian International Company (AIC). It is my pleasure to share with you our success story as steel work engineering, fabrication and erection experts. Since our founding in 1994 as part of the premier Attieh Group, our growth has been driven by the solid investment and strategic business focus of our parent company. Since then, AIC has earned accreditation for excellence in quality standards and service management. Our competitive edge lies in our commitment to providing a high-level of quality products and services along with the skills of our experienced engineers.

As we move forward to many more productive years, we promise to continue keeping the stringent industry standards while conducting our business with transparency, truthfulness, and honesty. Ultimately, it is our clients we value most and it is, therefore, our honor to dedicate this publication to you.

Our Vision

AIC aims to become the number one steel company around the globe, in terms of product quality, service efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

In our commitment to clients, we constantly seek to deliver an unparalleled level of customer satisfaction backed by a talented team who can provide dependable solutions to see a project through to completion.

Core Values

Aic Steel Fabrication

Structural steel

Complete engineering-to-erection for steel structures supporting oil and gas, industrial and commercial projects.

Plate work and piping

Design and fabrication of a multiplicity of products including pipes, stacks, industrial ducts, pressure vessels, heat exchangers, alongside other process equipment.

Pre-engineered building

WProperly designed, optimized and standardized steel buildings tailored to fit the intended purpose of the building while saving time and cost.

Sheet metal

Mass-production of a multiplicity of metal products including commercial ducts and cable trays, among others.

Storage tanks

The complete design, fabrication and site assembly of storage and water tanks of different diameters, servicing the oil and gas, industrial and utilities sectors.

Modules and skids

Optimizing the fabrication and assembly of complex structures off-site under safe and controlled plant conditions, including pipe-spools, process/ chemical skids and process/ pipe-rack modules.

Military
